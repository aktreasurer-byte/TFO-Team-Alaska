Why this matters

Alaska takes extra coordination—distance, weather, and access all add complexity. Fundraising directly supports:

  • Trip logistics & safety (planning, coordination, and support).
  • Gear & supplies so participants can show up and participate.
  • Outreach to reach Veterans, Service Members, and Gold Star families statewide.
  • Keeping trips no‑cost for those we serve.

Interested in sponsoring an item or donating services? Email ak.events@thefallenoutdoors.org.
