Why the banquet matters

This is our biggest annual fundraiser. The proceeds help keep trips no‑cost for participants and support the work it takes to run safe, well‑coordinated outdoor experiences across Alaska.

  • Trip logistics: transportation planning, location coordination, and safety support.
  • Gear & supplies: equipment support so people can show up and participate.
  • Outreach: connecting Veterans and Service Members to community and opportunity.
  • Future raffles & events: additional fundraising that directly fuels upcoming trips.
