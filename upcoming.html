How our trips work

Trip opportunities are posted on the TFO West Coast Facebook page. Each trip post includes entry instructions and deadlines.

TFO West Coast FB Banquet FB Page
  1. Watch for a trip post on the TFO West Coast Facebook page.
  2. Follow the instructions on that post if you’re interested in the trip.
  3. Winners will be drawn and notified.
  4. Participants are added to a group chat for trip details, meet-up info, and gear guidance.

If you’re not selected for a specific trip, keep watching—new opportunities are posted throughout the year.
