The Fallen Outdoors — Team Alaska

The Fallen Outdoors (TFO) is an all‑volunteer 501(c)(3) dedicated to connecting Veterans, Service Members, and Gold Star families through therapeutic outdoor adventures.

Our purpose

We use the outdoors to build camaraderie, reduce isolation, and help people reconnect—one trip at a time. Whether it’s a first fishing trip, a new hunting mentor, or simply time around a campfire, we focus on hope, connection, and healing.

  • Who we serve: Veterans, Active‑Duty Service Members, and Gold Star families.
  • What we do: No-cost outdoor adventures (hunting, fishing, hiking, camping, and more).
  • Why it matters: Community and purpose are protective factors—especially after service.

Our story

National founded 2009 • Team Alaska launched 2019

TFO began in 2009, founded by deployed soldiers who saw how powerful the outdoors can be for connection and recovery. Team Alaska started in 2019 to bring that same mission to the unique conditions and opportunities found here at home.

What makes Alaska different

Alaska is bigger, wilder, and more remote—so planning, safety, and logistics matter. It also offers unmatched opportunities for fishing and hunting that build confidence and lifelong skills.

Terrain & distance

Remote access, changing weather, and huge travel distances require strong coordination and experienced volunteers.

Fishing opportunities

From rivers to saltwater, fishing is one of our most accessible ways to get people outside and connected quickly.

Hunting mentorship

Hunting in Alaska can be life-changing—skill-building, challenging, and deeply rewarding when done safely and ethically.

Year-round community

We stay connected across seasons, using the outdoors as a consistent anchor for camaraderie.
