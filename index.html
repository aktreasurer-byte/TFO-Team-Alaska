How to join a trip

Trip opportunities are posted on the TFO West Coast Facebook group. This is a private group that helps us keep trip drawings focused on Veterans and Active Duty members and provides a secure place to share trip details.

  1. Request to join the TFO West Coast Facebook group.
  2. Answer the membership questions and follow the group rules so our team can verify eligibility and keep the community mission-focused.
  3. Watch for trip posts and follow the instructions on each post to enter.
  4. Winners are drawn and added to a group chat for meet-up info, safety notes, and gear guidance.
Request Access: TFO West Coast FB

If you’re not selected for one trip, keep watching—new opportunities are posted throughout the year.
